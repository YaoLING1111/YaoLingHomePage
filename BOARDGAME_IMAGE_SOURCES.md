# Board Game Image Sources

Product and storefront images used by the home-page board-game deck are stored in `public/img/boardgames/`.

- UNO: Mattel product page
- Avalon: Golden Goblin Games product page
- Blood on the Clocktower: official storefront
- Cheese Thief: Hoyle's of Oxford product page
- SCOUT: Greenfield Games / Oink Games product image
- CABO: Bezier Games article image
- One Night Ultimate Werewolf: Wolf Den product page
- Liar's Bar: Steam storefront
- Goose Goose Duck: Steam storefront
- Poker set: Escalade Sports product page
- ACQUIRE: Gamers Guild AZ / Renegade Game Studios product image

These images are used as identifying editorial thumbnails for the corresponding games. Traditional card-game entries without a single canonical box use locally rendered symbolic card artwork instead.
